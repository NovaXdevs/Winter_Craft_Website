Credits & Monetization

WinterCraft Reborn is a fork based on Luanti (formerly Minetest). All original licenses, copyrights, and credits are preserved.

  • The game is and will remain completely free & open source.
  • We listen to both the WinterCraft community and the Luanti community.
  • Monetization is limited to light ads (never during gameplay) and a €1/mo Premium for optional server features.

Ads may appear only on exit and before multiplayer join. No popups during gameplay.

Security contact: novaxdevs@gmail.com
